How do messages work?

You create a library of messages to play live in between songs or at certain dates and times. You have full control of which messages play and their frequency.

When can I start using messages?

If you upload pre-recorded messages they are available in your account right away. We require you to be out of the trial period to request messages that get recorded at our sound studio.

How do I record my own messages?

We have self-recording instructions available in our help articles here.

How many messages do you record?

We can record as many messages as you need. Studio recording costs are $99 per message. It will always be free to upload messages into your account that you have already pre-recorded.

How long before I get my messages?

It will take up to 5 business days for the studio messages to get recorded and loaded into your account. You will receive an email when they are ready to play.

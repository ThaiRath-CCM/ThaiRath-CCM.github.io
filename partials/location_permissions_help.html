Permissions Explanation

Music Permissions

Skip songs: Allows you to skip songs on the player. Skipping songs will only be allowed 6 times per hour.

Remove songs: When your location uses the web interface as the player, they can remove songs that they don't like.

Pause: Your location is allowed to pause the music

Management Permissions

Manage Music: Music managers can create and edit mix stations and dayparts (music schedules).

Manage Messages: Account message administrators can upload messages, and create and edit message presets and schedules.

Selection Permissions

Select Music: The location can select music from the available music lists.

Select Messages: The location can select message presets and schedules from the available lists.

Company Selections: The location will only see the music options you have chosen in the Company Selections tab.
